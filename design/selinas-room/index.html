I designed the portfolio site for my friend Selina, a computer science student. The concept centers around a girl working in a room, with the things around her representing the pages of the site.

fig 1.

Desktop view

Hovering over link buttons animates the background to focus over different parts of the room. The sprites also change slightly!

fig 2.

Day / night mode

The site has a toggle between day/night (or light/dark) mode, with a different background for the time of day. The default viewing mode is set based on the user’s real time.

fig 3.

JSON linking

Content for the site is loaded in via a JSON file, allowing edits to be made quickly and seamlessly.

fig 4.

Early draft

My first sketch of the concept! The idea randomly came to me in class, so it’s a page in my notes.
