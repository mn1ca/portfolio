Graphic design for CARE.

time  

2023-2025

type  

social media, print, merch.

tools 

Adobe Illustrator, Procreate
CARE is an organization at UC Davis dedicated to reducing the incidence and impact of sexual harassment and sexual violence, through advocacy, prevention programming, and training.

As the graphics student assistant, I was responsible for all design, including informational posts, event flyers, and merchandising.

The 'self-care' stickers were designed to be a collectible series at CARE events. Each features an act of self-care, and animals relevant to UC Davis campus.
